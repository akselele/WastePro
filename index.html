About


Save the world by donating plastic? That's possible!


By turning in your plastic you can either purchase a brick or see your good deed being put to good use.

Our goal


Involving everyone into recycling plastic


Plastic waste is a big problem. By involving everyone into being able to make a change, this problem can (not completely) be solved. You can bring in your own plastic waste, this includes plastics bags, foil, packages,...

You then have two choices: you can get your own recycled brick(s) at a discounted price or have the bricks being used for public infrastructure. This means YOU can see a direct change by just offering your plastic!

SERVICES

What we do


Collect plastic

Manufacture recycled plastic bricks

We manufacture bricks by using the collected plastic waste.



Sell recycled plastic bricks

We sell recycled plastic bricks to the people who donated plastic waste at a discounted price.

It is also possible to buy the bricks, but with no discount prices.

Build structures

We can build walls, ... from the recycled bricks.
